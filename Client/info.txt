--------- General ---------

Press arrow/num keys to move
Press [enter] to open chat box
Press [~] to open console
Press [tab] to open outfitter
Drag & drop to move items

--------- Spells / Commands ---------

Enter in chat box:
- exori vis
- exori mort
- /a [0-9]
- /up
- /down
- /temple
- /depot

--------- Requests ---------

Enter in console:
- set day
- set night
